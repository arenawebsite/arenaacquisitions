Investment Thesis

What we look for. Why it works.

01
Essential services with durable demand

Auto repair, trades, property services. Businesses people need in any economic environment. No turnaround bets — overlooked operators in resilient industries.

02
Underperformance with a clear cause

Absentee ownership, deferred systems, weak leadership. We look for businesses where the market position is solid but the management layer has underdelivered. That gap is our entry point.

03
SBA-eligible, real estate preferred

We structure through SBA 7(a) where possible, with preference for acquisitions that include real property. Business and CRE in one loan structure is where we have the most depth.
